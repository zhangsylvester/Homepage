Minnesota Research Workshop in Algebra & Combinatorics


MRWAC is a research workshop designed to foster collaboration within the combinatorics and algebra group at the University of Minnesota. It focuses on current graduate students and postdocs and Ph.D. alumni. Information about current and previous workshops can be found below, where an asterisk* indicates the organizer(s).

2020

The first edition (called "Combinatorics Working Groups") was held virtually in 2020 in order to fill the gap left by the GRWC which was canceled due to COVID-19.
